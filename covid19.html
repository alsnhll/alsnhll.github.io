Events

  • COVID-19 Dynamics & Evolution Conference (Co-Chair) - recurring scientific and public meetings focused on quantitative and computational approaches to studying SARS-CoV-2, including modeling virus transmission and control efforts, estimating epidemiological parameters, and phylogenetic analyses

  • COVID-19 Data Forum (Co-Chair) - recurring public webinarsfor topic experts to discuss data-related aspects of the scientific response to the pandemic
  • MIDAS Network (Member) - professional network and scientific meetings for researchers involved in mathematical modeling of infectious disease
